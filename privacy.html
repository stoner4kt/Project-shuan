ClearPath Finance Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how ClearPath Finance collects, uses, stores, and shares personal information when users submit a request through our website. We aim to align our practices with the Protection of Personal Information Act, 2013 (POPIA) in South Africa.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect information you provide directly, including your full name, email address, phone number, province, service preferences, optional identification details, and any message you include in your request.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to review your request, connect you with relevant third-party providers, communicate with you about your request, improve our services, and maintain appropriate records for operational, legal, and security purposes.

3. Sharing of Information

ClearPath Finance is a matching platform. To help you receive suitable options, we may share your information with trusted third-party providers, service partners, and service operators who assist with lead handling, communication, analytics, or hosting. We do not present ourselves as the provider of the financial product itself.

4. Legal Basis and Consent

By submitting a request through the website, you consent to the processing of your personal information for the purpose of matching you with a relevant provider and contacting you regarding your request. Where required, we rely on consent and legitimate business purposes consistent with applicable law.

5. Data Security

We use reasonable technical and organisational safeguards to protect information against loss, misuse, unauthorised access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction. However, no online transmission or storage method can be guaranteed as completely secure.

6. Retention

We retain personal information for as long as reasonably necessary to fulfil the purpose for which it was collected, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce our policies.

7. Your Rights

Subject to applicable law, you may request access to your personal information, correction of inaccurate data, deletion where appropriate, or further details regarding how your information has been used or shared.

8. Contact and Updates

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our services, legal requirements, or operational practices. Continued use of the website after updates may indicate acceptance of the revised policy.
