ClearPath Finance Legal Information

This page summarises important legal disclosures and general compliance statements relevant to the ClearPath Finance website and its role as a lead generation and matching service.

Important Disclosure

ClearPath Finance is not a lender or financial service provider. We connect users with third-party providers.

No Financial Advice

The content on this website is for general information and matching purposes only. It should not be treated as legal, credit, financial, insurance, or investment advice. Users should independently assess any offer they receive and seek professional advice where appropriate.

Partner Communication

By submitting your details, you may be contacted by a trusted partner about your request. Contact may occur by phone, email, SMS, or another communication channel relevant to your enquiry and consent.

Provider Responsibility

Any lending, debt consolidation, insurance, or related service is offered by an independent third-party provider. That provider is solely responsible for its product terms, pricing, approvals, customer service, and regulatory compliance obligations.

South African Compliance Context

ClearPath Finance aims to support responsible data handling in line with POPIA and to work with providers that operate in the appropriate South African regulatory environment. Users should verify the credentials and disclosures of any third-party provider before proceeding.

Website Availability

We may update, suspend, or modify the website and its forms at any time. We do not guarantee uninterrupted availability or that all information will always remain current, complete, or error-free.
